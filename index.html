About the University

Ramdeobaba University (RBU) is a premier educational institution located in Nagpur, India. Established in 1984, it has built a reputation for fostering academic excellence and nurturing student talent. With a strong focus on engineering and management, RBU equips students with the necessary skills to thrive in a competitive world.

The institution is known for its robust curriculum, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and a vibrant campus life. RBU continues to uphold its tradition of molding young minds into future leaders and innovators.
